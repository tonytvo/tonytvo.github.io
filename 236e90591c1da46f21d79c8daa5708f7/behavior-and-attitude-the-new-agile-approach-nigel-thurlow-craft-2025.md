# Behavior and attitude, the new Agile approach - Nigel Thurlow (Talk Outline)

> Nigel Thurlow (former Chief of Agile at Toyota, creator of *The Flow System*) on why we don't have a methodology problem — we have a behavior and leadership problem — and a rapid-fire, deliberately alarming AI tour.

---

## 1. The State of Agile

- No more certification/PDF factories, made-up roles, or the "transformation myth" (the Agile Manifesto beautified into sainthood — "it's just a philosophy"). **Culture is a product of behaviors.** We have plenty of methods; we need **better behavior, starting with leadership**.

## 2. Leadership Issues

- The reality of change: people earning **$3M–$45M/year don't have a problem** — the system works for them, so without significant fear/pain nobody changes. America "runs on incentives" (bonus, status, power), short horizons (OKRs, one to three months), and worships metrics (the **McNamara fallacy** — obsessing over data while ignoring contradicting information). **Leadership isn't a title — you earn it by building followers** (the test: "if you stopped paying them, would they still follow you?"). Lean/agile fails because orgs *desire* change but aren't *designed* for it, and leaders "are all right."

## 3. The AI Threat (fast and alarming)

- Timeline: transistor (1947) → AI (2017) → ChatGPT (2022) → agentic AI (2 years). Bengio: AI's task capability **doubles every ~7 months**; ~5 years from trouble. He (age 62, ex-COBOL) built a Christian-rock Disney song in **90 minutes** with Suno + LTX Studio, a Grok/HeyGen persona video, and had **Gemini write 800 error-free lines in 2 minutes** — "it's not *what* it wrote, it's the *fact* it wrote it." Yes it hallucinates, "but plenty of humans write bad code and we don't remove them" — it'll get better faster than us. **Agile coaches are "the dodo"** — evolve or go extinct.
- **Physical AI** = robotics + AI + sensors + world simulation: NVIDIA Omniverse/Cosmos + Accenture + KION warehouse **digital twins**, Wetnee laundry-folding robots, Waymo (20% of Austin rides, "no tip, doesn't text and drive"), Tesla Optimus, self-learning LLMs with **zero input data**, and Google **AlphaEvolve** writing/testing/debugging its own code ("AI doing agile by itself"). **Jidoka** (Sakichi Toyoda, ~100 years ago): "machines with a human touch" — never remove the human.

## 4. What to Do

- **Change behavior, not mindset** (John Shook: change how people behave by giving alternatives; "mindset" is pseudoscience — individuals have mindsets, groups don't; stop the "seductive allure of neuroscience"). Appeal to leaders' actual desires (P&L) via the **EAST** framework (Easy, Attractive, Social, Timely). New coaching roles: **ethical-risk coach, cyber-risk coach, project-turnaround specialist** (pull the andon cord — stop/slow, stabilize, kill variability, address tech debt and systemic constraints, weekly stakeholder reviews) — evolving toward **orchestrators/conductors** bridging worlds.
- **Agentic = giving power of attorney** — you outsource the decision. Dark note: autonomous weapons (Anduril's Roadrunner) and **human enfeeblement** (we forget how to do things AI does). Value comes from **skills beyond coding** (trades, judgment). Governance/law must control AI — "AI auditing AI" is already here.
