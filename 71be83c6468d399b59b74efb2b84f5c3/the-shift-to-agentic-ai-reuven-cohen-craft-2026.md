# The Shift to Agentic AI – Reuven Cohen (Talk Outline)

> Reuven Cohen frames his ~40-year career — from a software-less Tandy at age 8 to **agentic engineering** — as "coming full circle": modern agent work in a console looks like the Unix systems of the 1980s.

---

## 1. A Career of "Build and Share"

- Started with **shareware** (mail code to PC World to receive others' code) — a pre-open-source democratized culture. "Cyber designer" (1994), Napster UI (1999), then **Infrastructure-as-a-Service (2004)** open-sourced on SourceForge → AWS reached out → co-founded **CloudCamp** (300+ cities). The template: **put software into the ecosystem openly, and opportunities call you back.**

## 2. Compression of the Build Cycle

- Each wave — personal computing → web → mobile → **AI** — compresses idea-to-implementation. As CTO-level work at Microsoft and on a **$1.4B EY** AI infrastructure project, he saw enterprise builds shrink from months/years to a Claude Code session, **magnifying a single individual** to the output of a whole team. Tools shifted from *"a tool you call"* to *"a tool that calls you back."*

## 3. Agentic Engineering & the Harness

- **Agency** = systems that operate with little/no human oversight. From ~2022 (OpenAI VIP / Codex) he wrapped his build-test-deploy in an **autonomous harness** (**Claude Flow → Roo Flow**), running **swarms** of collaborating agents 24/7. Output: **>50M lines of code** — far too much for one human, forcing the discipline of **"understanding without seeing."** A background **"dream cycle"** mines recent research for novel, possible-but-never-done capabilities to build and share (Roo Flow ~50k stars, millions of weekly downloads; "GitHub is the social network, code is the content").

## 4. Contrastive Vector Intelligence (RuVector)

- Beyond building SaaS: **infuse AI into the physical world** (e.g., **RuView**, ~70k stars, Carnegie-Mellon-inspired — read heartbeats/pose from Wi-Fi signals). Built on a **contrastive** approach: model **what isn't happening** (the null/void), so monitoring 8 billion people collapses to the ~20k anomalies — huge power/cost savings, runs on an **ESP32**, no GPU/cloud.
- **RuVector stack:** **hyperbolic vectors** (a center point expanding in all directions → native hierarchy, vs. a flat Euclidean plane) + a **hypergraph** overlay + a **graph neural network** (attention/reasoning). **Deterministic math over probabilism** — for medical/critical use you want *"I don't know"* or reproducible steps, not the most-probable answer. Runs in **sublinear (<1 ms) loops** → less energy, less entropy/noise → real-time signal separation; the **human is the highest-order model** (escalate to another agent/person/tool as needed).

## 5. Sovereignty & Close

- A deliberate shift from the cloud/online world (he ran a BBS as a kid) to **sovereign, owned, network-independent AI** — the future balances a few hyperscalers against individuals building full stacks they control. His company **Cognitum** infuses this **ambient, GPU-free AI** into business/IoT/medical devices that **learn in proximity** and act **proactively before something matters**. The real lesson isn't the 50M lines — it's doing things in the **least, cleanest, most efficient code**. He sees AI not as job loss but **reinvention** — agents as augmentation of human capability.
